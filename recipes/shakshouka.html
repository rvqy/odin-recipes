Shakshouka

Description

Shakshuka is one of the tastiest ideas for a healthy breakfast. The recipe is incredibly simple, and shakshuka is super delicious and satisfying. It's a combination of eggs and tomatoes at its best.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 small red bell pepper
  • 2 tomatoes
  • A pinch (or more) of chili flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt, pepper
  • A pinch of sugar
  • Sweet paprika powder
  • A handful of chopped parsley
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Dice the bell pepper and onion into small cubes. Blanch the tomatoes, peel them, and also dice them into cubes. Press the garlic through a garlic press.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté the onion until translucent. Add the garlic and sauté for a little while longer.
  3. Add the bell pepper, tomatoes, and all the spices. Stir and simmer until the tomatoes break down. Adjust the seasoning if necessary.
  4. When a thick tomato sauce forms, create two wells in the sauce. Crack the eggs into the wells, season them with salt and pepper.
  5. Cover the pan and fry until the egg whites are set.
  6. Sprinkle the dish with parsley and serve with your favorite bread.
