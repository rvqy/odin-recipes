Shakshouka

Description

Shakshuka is one of the tastiest ideas for a healthy breakfast. The recipe is incredibly simple, and shakshuka is super delicious and satisfying. It's a combination of eggs and tomatoes at its best.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 small red bell pepper
  • 2 tomatoes
  • A pinch (or more) of chili flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt, pepper
  • A pinch of sugar
  • Sweet paprika powder
  • A handful of chopped parsley
  • Olive oil
